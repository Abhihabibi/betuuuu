Our Story ❤️

Humari kahani koi normal si kahani nahi hai, betuuu… 💖

Pehli baar jab hum mile the online, tujhe yaad hai? Raat thi, tu akeli ghar pe thi aur thoda darr bhi laga tha. Tab humne woh pehla video call kiya tha 🌙. Aur uss raat se lekar subah tak hum baatein karte rahe… aur sach batau toh, mujhe laga yeh ladki toh alag hi hai ✨.

Tabse humari aadat ban gayi late night talks ki. Roz ki baaton mein hasi bhi hai, roothna bhi hai, aur woh pyaar jo shabdon se kabhi bayan hi nahi ho sakta 🥺. Main tujhe "Betuu" aur "Chikki babu" bulata hoon… aur woh naam sirf hum dono ki duniya ke hain. 🤍

🌹 *Shayari for you, meri jaan:* 🌹
*"Tere bina adhura tha main, teri muskaan se roshan hua, raat bhar baatein jo hoti hai humari, usi mein mera saara jahan basa hua."* 💕

Aastha, hum apne rishtay ko naam nahi dete… kyunki shayad har rishta chhota pad jaye uske saamne jo hum feel karte hain. Bas itna kehna hai: tu meri aadat hai, meri dua hai, mera sab kuch hai. 🤍
